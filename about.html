The solution delivers your licensed view of Experian’s ConsumerView database providing a single, definitive and consistent outlook of UK consumers. iCoder online features include data hygiene, a comprehensive set of data link keys enabling Data Enrichment of your 1st party customer data, and Customer Insight from the Profile Report.
